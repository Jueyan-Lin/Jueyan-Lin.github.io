Food market research

This is the recent group project for one of the top food company.
We did related market research about the potential market in China.

  • ¥3,000T Package Food Market Size
  • 13% General Growth Rate
  • 20% Organic Food Growth Rate
  • 90% Online Shopping under 60
  • ¥300+ Willing to purchase per item

Our research focus on package food market for the elder (aged over 50). The report describes our research in four parts, current market, customer analysis, competition products and our recommendation about the new products. The overall market for elderly people is growing rapidly, and the potential market is also large, growing fast and stable. The key market drivers include aging problem, consumer behavior changing and rising demand for organic food.
